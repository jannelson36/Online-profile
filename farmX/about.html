Linel Poultry Farm

Linel Poultry Farm provides to the community, all grain well fed chickens which are handled and processed by the farm. Alongside fresh and frozen poultry, the farm additionally has a collection of different items they sell.
Our chickens are raised on stable floors. These stables are well ventilated and are kept at an agreeable temperature for our poultry. We feed our chickens whole grain diet that is specially mixed by us. There are no development energizers or stimulants included or any animal byproduct in this feed. There is additionally a steady supply of fresh water to all the stables. We measure poultry utilizing simply fresh water.

In an industry that is continually changing, our numerous years of experience and versatility has permited our process and product offering to meet our customers’ and the end market’s ceaselessly changing neccessities.
